About Me
I am the 45th and current president of the United States. Before entering politics, I was a businessman and television personality. I was born and raised in Queens, a borough of New York City, and received a bachelor's degree in economics from the Wharton School. I took charge of my family's real-estate business in 1971, renamed it The Trump Organization, and expanded its operations from Queens and Brooklyn into Manhattan. The company built or renovated skyscrapers, hotels, casinos, and golf courses. Next I started various side ventures, mostly by licensing my name. I produced and hosted The Apprentice, a reality television series, from 2003 to 2015. As of 2020, Forbes estimated my net worth to be $2.1 billion
